Hey, I'm Aditi.
// not just a stack of technologies

🎨 Beyond the Terminal

I paint. Like, actually sit down with colours and let my mind go completely blank. There's something about watching colours blend that no debugger can replicate. Dog person, always. Currently trying to convince myself I need another plant.

📚 Always Reading Something

Mostly fiction. I disappear into books the way some people disappear into Netflix. I also keep a Notion page full of quotes, random thoughts, and phrases that hit differently at 11pm. Yes, I journal. Inconsistently, but with full commitment when I do.

💻 The Honest Dev Life

Not a night owl and I don't function after midnight. I code until 2am anyway. Stack Overflow in 7 tabs, that one console.log that somehow fixes everything. Still fighting DP problems and choosing to believe a miracle is coming.

🌤 How I Move Through Life

I genuinely believe every dark cloud has a silver lining. Not as a cliche, but as a choice. I want to be the kind of person who finds the light and stands in it, the kind of person who believes in "the grass is greener where i stand". Family, friends, good conversations, good food. That's the real stack.

THINGS I BELIEVE
  • Readable code is a gift to everyone who comes after you, including yourself at 3am.
  • "It works on my machine" is a confession, not a solution.
  • Writing tests feels like a waste of time until the one moment it isn't.
  • Good documentation is the last act of kindness to future-you. Do it anyway.
  • Done and imperfect will always beat perfect and stuck in your head.
  • The best engineers I know are the most curious ones, not the smartest ones.
